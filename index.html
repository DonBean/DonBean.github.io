US War Map

A map of US military conflicts across the world

This comprehensive spreadsheet documents the history of war in the United States, including officially recognized conflicts, US Congress declared wars, and other military actions taken by the US government. From the Revolutionary War to the present day, this chart outlines the various conflicts and interventions in which the United States has been involved. This project is a work in progress, and will be updated as new information becomes available. It serves as a valuable resource for understanding the role of the United States in global conflicts and military actions throughout history.

13 Keys to Predicting the Next President

A document detailing the lineage and history of the US Supreme Court.

The "13 Keys to the White House" is a system for predicting the outcome of U.S. presidential elections. Developed by Professor Allan Lichtman and Vladimir Keilis-Borok, the system consists of 13 true/false statements that are used to determine the likelihood of the incumbent party winning the election. The keys are based on various factors, including economic indicators, policy changes, social unrest, and foreign or military failures. Lichtman has been correctly predicting elections with this model since 1984.
